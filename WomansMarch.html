WOMANS MARCH 2017

In January 2017, 2.6 million people around the world participated in woman marches. The protest united people of all ages, sex, races, religion, ex. It was looked at as a revolution against wrongful human and civil rights.

Womans Rights

Womens movement refers to a series of campains for issues such as reproductive rights, domestic violence, meternity leave, equal pay, women sufferage, sexual harassment, and violence

LGBT+ Rights

Support lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, ex., rights. People around the world face violence, inequality, and as far as torture and execution just because of who they love. Kids get made fun of for having gay parents. Queer people can be kicked out of church. Kids can be kicked out of their house for coming out. And even until recently being transgender meant no helth care. People apart of this movement fight for things to change in the way society treats and looks at the LGBT+ community.

Disability Rights

The Disability Rights Movement fights for rights for people with disabilities. They believe people with disabilities should have equal access to rights, oppritunities, and services, while being protected from discrimination, abuse, and neglect

Religion

People who suopport this movement believe no one should be discriminated or face predjudice because of their faith or religion.

Civil Rights

Civil Rights Movement fights for equal rights no matter your skin color. This movement works against discrimination and predjudice in society and teach to treat people equally no matter their race or ethnicity.
